Workflow

How Scribe works in your day

1

Patient enters the room

Tap "Start Recording" in the Arogya app or EHR widget. Scribe begins ambient listening. You can pause and restart the recording anytime.

2

AI processes in real time

Large language models transcribe, extract clinical entities, and structure the conversation into note format. Stop recording once the visit is done.

Review the draft note

🌿 About Arogya Labs

Building AI that gives physicians time to heal

Arogya means "health" in Sanskrit. We started this company because we watched brilliant physicians spend 40% of their day on documentation — not patients. We're changing that.

Mission

AI that augments, not replaces, the clinician

We believe the physician-patient relationship is sacred. Our AI exists to handle the administrative and analytical tasks that pull physicians away from that relationship — documentation, imaging triage, coding, reporting.

Every product we build is designed with a "physician in the loop" philosophy. AI provides the draft, the insight, the alert — the clinician makes the final call.
